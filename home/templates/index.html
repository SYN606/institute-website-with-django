Career Oriented Courses

Karamraji provides a list of courses for the students like IT Scholars, Short Term Courses, Career Oriented Courses and Govt. Approved Courses.

'O' Level

Duration : 12 Months
Eligibility : 12th Passed

A Scheme of AICTE & Department of IT Govt. of India
DOEACC ‘O’ Level Course is a foundation Course of DOEACC Society in the field of Information Technology.

CCC (Course on Computer Concepts)

Duration : 2 Months
Eligibility : 8th Passed

A Scheme of AICTE & Department of IT Govt. of India
CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) course is launched by NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) and they are designed to impart a basic level IT Literacy to the common man with more emphasis on practical training. These Courses are basically designed for 8th Standard passed students.

ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications)

Duration : 12 Months
Eligibility : 12th Passed

A Professional Diploma Course for Web/Graphic Designer and Jr. Software Engineer
Advance Diploma in Computer Application is a 2 semester course and is designed to take students from basics of computer to the Complexities of Developing Software Applications. This course make students comfortable with office automation, Graphic Designing, and enhance programming skill requires in the IT industry. The aim of the course is to produce efficient computer programmers within short duration to fulfil huge requirement of IT industry.

DCA (Diploma in Computer Applications)

Duration : 6 Months
Eligibility : 10th Passed

Diploma Program for Graphic Designer and Web Designer
Diploma in Computer Application is course to build career as a Teacher/Banker/ Designer of information technology.
