ADCA

Duration : 12 Months

Eligibility : 12th Passed

A Professional Diploma Course for Web/Graphic Designer and Jr. Software Engineer.

Advance Diploma in Computer Application is a 2 semester course and is designed to take students from basics of computer to the Complexities of Developing Software Applications. This course make students comfortable with office automation, Graphic Designing, and enhance programming skill requires in the IT industry. The aim of the course is to produce efficient computer programmers within short duration to fulfil huge requirement of IT industry. The technical modules of the course will provide industry standard domain knowledge to the students and help them in the competitive environment.

ADCA Course Syllabus - Per Semester :

  1. ADCA Sem - 1 - Duration : 06 Months
    • Computer Fundamentals
    • Microsoft Office 2007
    • Libre Office
    • Internet & Email
    • Computer Network & Multimedia Concept
  2. ADCA Sem - 2 - Duration : 06 Months
    • Tally 5.4
    • Visual Basic
    • C Programming
    • C++ Programming
    • Coral Draw
    • Photoshop
