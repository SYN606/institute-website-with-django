CCC

Duration : 2 Months

Eligibility : 8th Passed

A Scheme of AICTE & Department of IT Govt. of India

CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) course is launched by NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) and they are designed to impart a basic level IT Literacy to the common man with more emphasis on practical training. These Courses are basically designed for 8th Standard passed students.

Syllabus for CCC :

  • Introduction to Computer
  • Introduction to GUI Based Operating System
  • Elements of Word Processing(LibreOffice Writer)
  • Spreadsheets Concept of Ms-Excel
  • Introduction to Internet, WWW and Web browsers
  • Communication and Collaboration
  • Application of Digital Financial Services
  • Application of Presentations(LibreOffice Impress)
