About Institute

Karmraji institute of Computer Science & IT (KICS & IT) is under the management of Karamraji Thakurdeen Smarak, Sikshan Sansthan,(KTSSS) which stands registered with U.P. Govt. Administration under Act 21 , 1860. The compliances of the sansthan is non-sectarian, its main object is to spread education and provide employment to the unemployed.
The motto of the sansthan is “Servire Deo Est Servite Homini” which means “SERVICE TO HUMANITY IS SERVICE TO GOD”. The institute aims at spreading computer education among the poor and socially backward sections of the society. The institute run on no profit no loss basis. Karamraji Institute of Computer Science & IT (KICS & IT) is Accrediated By Department of Electronics Accreditation of Computer Courses (DOEACC) with Full ACCR No. 01733 offered DOEACC ‘O’ LEVEL Computer Course. DOEACC Society (An ISO 9001-2000 certified body) a joint Scheme of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Department of Information Technology (DIET).
